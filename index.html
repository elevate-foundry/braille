About SymbiOS

SymbiOS is a cross-platform system that integrates Apple and Android devices, focusing on mirroring a Samsung phone to a Mac and accessing a Samsung watch through that mirror. The system includes features for sending haptic messages in braille, enhancing accessibility and user experience.
