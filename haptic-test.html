About Biological Haptic Mode

The Biological Contractions mode mimics the rhythmic patterns of physiological contractions, making braille contractions more intuitive to learn through haptic feedback.

This mode creates a wave-like pattern that builds up, peaks, and tapers off - similar to natural biological contractions. For braille contractions, it uses a series of three waves with increasing intensity to emphasize the contraction nature.
