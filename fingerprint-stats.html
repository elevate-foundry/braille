Our Privacy Commitment

We prioritize your privacy while providing enhanced security through fingerprinting. Our approach:

  • No personal information is ever collected or stored
  • All data is anonymized and cannot be traced back to individuals
  • We focus on patterns, not content - what you type or view remains private
  • You maintain full control over your data
  • Fingerprinting is used to protect your account from unauthorized access
